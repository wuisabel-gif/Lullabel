# Code of Conduct 💛

Lullabel is about names real people will carry for a lifetime, drawn from living cultures and faith traditions. Everyone who takes part — contributors, reviewers, and maintainers — is expected to help keep this a kind, respectful, and welcoming project.

## Our standards

**We expect everyone to:**

- Treat others with respect, patience, and good faith.
- Welcome newcomers and help them contribute.
- Represent every culture, language, and faith tradition honestly and without stereotype, mockery, or caricature.
- Give and accept feedback graciously, keeping discussion constructive.
- Respect differing viewpoints and experiences.

**The following are not welcome:**

- Slurs, hate speech, or demeaning comments about any group, religion, nationality, or identity.
- Offensive, mocking, or "joke" name entries, or contributions that disparage the cultures names come from.
- Personal attacks, harassment, or intimidation of any kind.
- Sustained disruption of discussions or reviews.

## Scope

This applies to all project spaces — pull requests, issues, reviews, commit messages, and any other community interaction connected to the project.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it privately to the maintainer via their GitHub profile, [@wuisabel-gif](https://github.com/wuisabel-gif).
*(Maintainers: consider adding a contact email here so reports can reach you directly.)*

Reports will be reviewed and handled as confidentially as the situation allows.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, close, or remove contributions, comments, or other content that violates this Code of Conduct, and may warn or block anyone who behaves unacceptably. Decisions aim to be fair, proportionate, and focused on keeping the community safe and welcoming.

In short: bring names you'd be proud to give, and treat others the way you'd want to be treated.
